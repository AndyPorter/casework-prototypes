Verify relationship with your solicitor or conveyancer

To verify your relationship you must enter the unique code generated by the solicitor or conveyancer acting on your behalf.

If you haven’t been given your code, or have lost it, contact your solicitor or conveyancer.

Once you have entered the code on GOV.UK your relationship will be verified and your solicitor or conveyancer notified.

To use this service you’ll need to:

  • confirm your identity on GOV.UK
  • enter the unique code from your solicitor or conveyancer
  • confirm the address of the property or land and what you want to do with it
